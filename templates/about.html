About Us


Our aim was to create a website for the purpose of our mini project and WPL lab.

Objectives-

  1. The aim is to predict and detect the possibility of lung cancer using a machine learning approach via health factors and/or scans (images).
  2. To provide AI assistance to the medical industry.
  3. To provide an interactive user interface by means of web programming for the analysis of the same.
  4. To inform masses about lung cancer disease and treatments.
