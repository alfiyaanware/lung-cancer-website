Treatments


The common types of treatments used for early-stage and locally advanced lung cancer are:

  1. Surgery
    • Wedge resection: to remove a small section of lung that contains the tumor along with a margin of healthy tissue.
    • Segmental resection: to remove a larger portion of lung, but not an entire lobe
    • Lobectomy: to remove the entire lobe of one lung
    • Pneumonectomy: to remove an entire lung
  2. Radiation therapy
  3. Chemotherapy
  4. Stereotactic body radiotherapy
  5. Targeted drug therapy
  6. Immunotherapy
  7. Palliative care
  8. Systemic therapy concerns for people age 65 or older
  9. Physical, emotional, and social effects of cancer
  10. Recurrent breast cancer
